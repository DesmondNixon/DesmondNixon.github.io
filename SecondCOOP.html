The HUNGIE project

In lieu of a work placement during my second Cooperative term, I worked on a couple side projects, my main one being the HUNGIE project.

The HUNGIE project was started with the idea and intention to provide those with dietary restrictions, intolerances, and/or prefferences a comprehensive list of places to eat that match their individual criteria. The project consists of a website landing page (linked below) as well as a companion app which is still in development.
